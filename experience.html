Experience

An afternoon
with clay

Hand-modeling workshops in the Balat atelier. No wheel, no hurry — just clay, your hands, and tea served in cups that were made exactly this way.

Small groups, slow hands

Sessions are limited to six guests and last around two and a half hours. You will pinch, coil and press a piece of your own; we dry, fire and glaze it at 1240 °C over the following weeks.

Finished pieces can be collected at the studio or shipped worldwide in our plastic-free packaging.

How it unfolds
i.

Wedging

We start at the bench — pushing the air out of the clay until it moves like something alive.

ii.

Hand-modeling

No wheel. Pinch pots, coils, slabs — the oldest techniques there are, taught at a Balat pace.

iii.

The firing

Your piece joins the studio kiln at 1240 °C. We send a photo the morning it comes out.

Pre-registration

Reserve an afternoon

Sessions run Thursday to Sunday. Studio visits otherwise by appointment.
